Winning by Jack Welch

This is the best book ever I have read. Jack Welch is a former CEO of general electric. He is famous for his strcit attitude in leadership. During his period, GE market value increase from $12bn to more than $400bn. He wrote this book not to make profit, and I think this is a key differene with the casual success books in the market. The book starts with a spectacular opening, when Welch defines his own interpretation of winning, and says why you should win. The book has a lot of key factors of how to be a good leader in your organization. However, I think most of them are applicable to our everyday lives. For instance, Welch emphesizes on having candor. He says that only candor can eliminate killing bureaucracies within an organizations. Candor speeds up processes, removes misunderstandings, and increses achievements. I can roughly devide my life into two parts. The days before trying candor and the days after. I believe that my biggest achievements are from the time I started to use candor in my everyday life. It had great impacts both on my career and personal life. You should read the book!
