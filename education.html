Education

MSc Electrical and Computer Engineering, 2019

Ferwodsi University of Mashhad, Iran

During my MSc program, I took a veriety of courses concerning software and hardware of processing architectures. In software related domain, I had advanced computer architecture and fualt tolerant systems design. I used what I leart in both courses directly in my collaborations with industrial R&D labs (for more information see projects). My thesis dealt with a study on heterogeneous FPGA architectures. Its goal was to find an optimum configuration of a soft-core processor without doing the actual synthesis and implementation steps on hardware.

BSc Electrical and Computer Engineering, 2016

Ferdowsi University of Mashhad, Iran

My BSc was my first step to realize the underneath layers of the mysterious technology. There were lots of courses in analog and digital design. Although all of the courses sound amazing, I found myself interested in digital domain. My final project was implementation of a simple data trasmitter over visible light.
